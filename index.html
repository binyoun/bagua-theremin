Bagua Theremin

A theremin played across the Later Heaven Bagua. Your right hand's position on the wheel picks colour and pitch, gliding continuously — no fixed notes. Your left hand's height controls volume. With only one hand up, it plays at a quiet default; bring the other in to shape it.

Uses your camera locally, on-device. Nothing is recorded or uploaded.
