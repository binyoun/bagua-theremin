Bagua Theremin

A bagua mirror floats over your camera feed. Your right hand's position on it picks colour and pitch, gliding continuously — no fixed notes. Your left hand's height controls volume. With only one hand up, it plays at a quiet default; bring the other in to shape it.

Uses your camera locally, on-device. Nothing is recorded or uploaded.

Right hand: position on the disc = pitch and colour · Left hand: height = volume
